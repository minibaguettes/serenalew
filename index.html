Hello there! I'm Serena, a computer science graduate at UC Riverside.

I would spend hours lost in another world and found it exciting. In high school, I discovered that I also enjoyed playing tennis and gaming, which stuck with me through university. Gaming was the gateway to my interest in programming, which led me to major in computer science.


Currently, I am working on a few different projects that allow me to learn new languages and methods, mainly Ruby and CSS. One of the main projects is for Artr, a start up with a few friends with a goal in mind: making commissions easier for artists and clients. Learning web development had always been in my sights, so this project really helped me get my foot in. That being said, I am strongly interested in web development, software development, and quality assurance, but I am more than happy to dive into other fields and expand my skills. I am seeking an entry level position where I can make a great impact and keep learning new things.
